RoBust

RoBust is a web application that allows CIT – U passengers to select and reserve a bus seat by themselves. With this, the CIT – U passengers could reserve a seat without physically going to the IMDC office to check its availability. Aside from that, the application allows these CIT – U passengers to check the latest update on scheduled time departure of the bus, information and tickets in a particular day and time, allowing them to experience a convenient bus seat reservation ahead of time. The goal of this system is to reduce the conventional paper ticketing method and to provide a new way for bus ticketing via virtual process. However, this system is not limited to over-the-counter walk-in CIT – U passengers.
